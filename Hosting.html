WHAT IS HOSTING?

THE FOUNDATION OF YOUR WEBSITE

Hosting is an online service that allows you to publish your website or web application on the Internet. When you buy hosting, you rent space on a server where all your website’s files, databases, and resources are stored securely and made available 24/7.

Hosting is essential for every website. Without it, your domain name won’t have any content to display. There are several types of hosting:

  • Shared Hosting: Affordable, ideal for beginners and small websites.
  • VPS Hosting: More powerful and customizable for growing sites.
  • Dedicated Hosting: Full control of your own server — for large enterprises.
  • Cloud Hosting: Flexible and scalable, perfect for websites that need speed and reliability.
